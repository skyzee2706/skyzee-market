# SKY Market

**SKY Market** is a decentralized prediction market platform running on the Ethereum Sepolia testnet. Users bet on whether **BTC/USD will close above or below a target price** at a specific end time.

Markets run on a fully automated on-chain schedule — no manual action required for market creation or resolution.

---

## Why SKY Market?

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Transparent** | All bets, payouts, and resolutions are on-chain and verifiable |
| **Automated** | Bot creates and resolves markets without human input |
| **Fair** | Betting closes before settlement to prevent manipulation |
| **Real-Time** | Live BTC/USD chart with WebSocket tick data and 10-CEX median price |
| **Free to Try** | Claim free SkyUSDT from the in-app faucet |
